How It Works

When you login from Desktop your meeting room will look like this

This is your Audio and Video pane where you will see Council Members and Managing Agent.

This is where you find information of your login , your name, device you are using and the operating System

Click 3 dots on right hand top corner to view network strength and language options

This is where all presentation material will be shown, it is possible that this is toggled with the Audio and Video broadcast

Click the expand icon to view a presentation in full screen mode

If you have a question or you want to speak, raise hand, you will be given permission to speak at which time you will be requested to switch on your audio and/or video and once switched on , you will be audible and/or visible to all participants

Check out your audio and video settings by clicking the gear icon in Audio and Video Panel

Test your video and Audio settings, if you intend to speak during the meeting

This is the voting panel

If allowed during the meeting you can post questions here

Whenever a resolution is being discussed you will see it here

See details of the resolution

See how many shares and units you are voting for as an Owner

If you are holding proxies or are attending as a proxy see the shares held here

If SP who has given proxy to you has already indicated a decision you will see the decision here

Whenever Voting starts for a resolution you will be able to view the actions available to you for the resolution here

View Resolutions for which decisions have been made here

How It Works On Mobile

When you login from your mobile your meeting room will look like this

Your default opening window will be the video window where you can view all presenters when the presenters video is streamed.

The Raise hand feature is available on the video window, If you have a question or you want to speak, raise hand

When you are given permission to speak at which time you will be requested to switch on your audio and/or video and once switched on , you will be audible and/or visible to all participants

Your device will request for permission, click Allow.

Please note that if you click NO or Cancel , such settings will be saved on your browser preferences and you will not be able to provide access of your camera or mic for the meeting unless you enable access from the settings menu of your device by providing a minimum of Ask permission OR by resetting your browser preferences and clearing the website history.
